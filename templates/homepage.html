ABOUT US

Kashiyatra, the Annual Socio-Cultural festival of IIT (BHU) Varanasi is a three day fiesta where participants from all over the country compete in the fields of literature, music and arts to find the best amongst themselves. Its grandeur has earned it the fame of being North India's one of the largest cultural festival.
IIT (BHU) Varanasi rests amidst the sanctity of Asia's biggest residential university, Banaras Hindu University, in the holy city of Varanasi. It is a nationally acclaimed institute for imparting technical knowledge in diverse fields of engineering.
